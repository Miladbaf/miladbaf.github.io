About Me

I'm a recent graduate with a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Sabanci University (CGPA: 3.73/4.0, top 8%), and currently completing a double major in Electronics Engineering with a minor in Physics.

My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, deep learning, and wireless communications. I've contributed to cutting-edge research on LLM agents, mechanistic interpretability, and FET modeling, resulting in publications at top-tier venues including ICML and SMACD.

Before returning to academia, I spent several years as an AI Product Manager, where I led teams to develop innovative AI-driven products that reached thousands of users. This unique combination of research depth and industry experience allows me to bridge theoretical advancements with practical applications.
